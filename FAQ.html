Frequently Asked Questions

General

What types of rice does Jovita's Rice Store offer?

We offer a wide variety of rice, including Japanese rice, Premium rice, Brown rice, Red rice, Basmati rice, and Black rice, as well as Pusa Sella Basmati Rice.

Where do you source your rice?

Our rice is sourced from local farms in the provinces of Isabela, Mindoro, and Nueva Ecija. We also offer premium international varieties from Vietnam, Japan, and Thailand.

Services

Do you offer custom rice packaging?

Yes, we provide flexible and customized packaging solutions, including bulk packaging and private labeling.

Do you offer shipping or delivery?

Yes, we offer both local and bulk shipping options to ensure timely and reliable delivery.

Do you offer rice milling services?

Yes, we provide professional rice milling services for local farmers and producers.

Orders and Policies

What is your return policy?

Our return policy allows you to return items within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.

How can I track my order?

You can track your order using the tracking number provided in your order confirmation email.
