The Mission and Vision of Jovita's Rice Store

Our History

Jovita's Rice Store was founded in 1980 by Mrs. Vita Mercado in the heart of Alabang, Muntinlupa, Philippines. After inheriting the business, Ms. Catherine Mercado took on the responsibility of managing and continuing the legacy built by her mother. From its humble beginnings, the store quickly gained a reputation for offering the finest quality rice sourced directly from local farms. The rice comes from the provinces of Isabela, Mindoro, and Nueva Ecija, ensuring the best produce from trusted local farmers. Additionally, premium international rice varieties from Vietnam, Japan, and Thailand are offered, catering to a wide range of customer preferences.

Our Mission

At Jovita's Rice Store, the mission is to provide the best quality rice products to customers while supporting local rice farmers. The goal is to offer an affordable and sustainable rice supply for Filipino families, restaurants, and businesses. The store is committed to promoting responsible farming practices and contributing to the growth of the Philippine rice industry.

Our Humble Beginnings

Mrs. Vita Mercado, the founder, started the business with just a small stall selling quality rice to local communities.

Our Vision

The vision for Jovita's Rice Store is a future where it is synonymous with quality and trust. The goal is to expand its reach throughout the Philippines and even internationally, becoming a leader in the rice industry while continuously supporting sustainable agricultural practices. The store strives to be the go-to place for all rice needs, known for integrity, quality, and customer-focused service.

The Future of Jovita's Rice Store

The store aspires to expand nationwide and reach international markets, helping more people enjoy high-quality rice.
